JUSTIN BRÛLOTTE

What is your biggest fear? Mine is to die knowing that I did not do my best helping others. From as far as I can remember, I have always wanted to help people get better and happier. This is also what pushes me to accomplish things most people think are impossible. As an example; when I was only 17, I founded my own web site programming business while starting and leading from scratch a robotics team with a friend in high school. My implication was recognized with the creation of a prize by our names. The prize “Brulotte-Morin” is now handed to new robotic enthusiasts annually in honor of our work. My passion for robotic lead me to become president of the only University driven robotics mentoring clubs in Canada. I am responsible for over 80 mentors and impacting more than 500 kids. Meanwhile I have also been named VP of logistics in 2018 and President in 2019 for the Oktoberfest the biggest thematic party in Canada. Managing more than 100 volunteers at the event and coordinating staff and supplies with a budget of no less than 500 000$. My name is Justin Brûlotte, I’m in my graduate in Computer Engineering at the University of Sherbrooke. I consider myself as an entrepreneur and athlete (I arrived 18th in the last Canadian Lifeguard-swimming competition). I currently operate my own freelance web business and a startup, Evenix, in event tracking with NFC. Let chat !
