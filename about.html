JUSTIN BRÛLOTTE

My name is Justin Brûlotte. I build software products across AI, DevOps, SaaS, event technology, robotics, and Web3.

I currently work on AI and MLOps systems while continuing to build products as a founder. I am the founder and full-stack developer behind Evenix, an event operations platform for contactless entry, attendance tracking, emergency workflows, and smart wristband experiences. I am also co-founder and VP Software at ARTIK, where I work on practical mobile robotics.

My projects usually start from a concrete operational problem: helping organizers run safer events, helping buyers find the right car, helping local businesses explore new payment models, or making robotics easier to use outside a lab. I care about shipping useful systems, not only prototypes.

Before these current projects, I studied computer engineering at the University of Sherbrooke, founded a web programming business at 17, helped build and lead robotics teams, and organized large student events with more than 100 volunteers and major logistics budgets.
